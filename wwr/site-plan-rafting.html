Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to make ourselves better known, to present our work and to be able to have more clients.

Audience

Our audience is both men and women, it can be of various ages but mainly adults, some questions to ask are: who would use this site? A: It can be used by both men and women of any age, but mainly adults, both tourists and locals. Where would you view the site? A: Due to the variety of audiences, I believe that most of them view it on their cell phones. What would people view the site for? A: I think they are looking for a different experience, many will be tourists and people who want to know a little more about this experience.
